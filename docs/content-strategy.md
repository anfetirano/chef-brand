# Content Strategy

## Content objective

Every page should help a serious employer move from curiosity to confidence. Content is not there to decorate the product or fill sections. It exists to reduce uncertainty and strengthen professional trust.

## Home

### Purpose

Create the first impression and frame Andres immediately as a high-standard chef worth interviewing.

### Content role

- Positioning statement
- Short narrative hook
- Core trust signals
- Immediate professional context
- Clear next actions

### Key message

This is not simply a chef profile. This is a credible professional with discipline, identity, and international potential.

## About

### Purpose

Explain who Andres is as a person and professional.

### Content role

- Personal story
- Values
- Culinary philosophy
- Professional attitude
- Identity beyond job titles

### Key message

Andres is someone with a real point of view, standards, and character.

## Experience

### Purpose

Turn career history into meaningful proof.

### Content role

- Career journey
- Important roles
- Skills developed
- Leadership and responsibility signals
- Evidence of growth and readiness

### Key message

Experience is not just a list of workplaces. It is proof of consistency, learning, and capability.

## Gallery

### Purpose

Provide visual trust signals.

### Content role

- Curated food photography
- Professional context
- Signals of precision and standards
- Moments that support quality perception

### Key message

The gallery should show care, execution, and taste, not random image accumulation.

## Resume

### Purpose

Offer structured proof for serious evaluators.

### Content role

- Concise credentials
- Employment timeline
- Skills overview
- Certifications or formal background
- Download options

### Key message

The resume is supporting evidence within a stronger story system.

## Contact

### Purpose

Convert trust into action.

### Content role

- Direct contact methods
- Employer-friendly messaging
- Clear invitation to connect
- Opportunity-focused framing

### Key message

Reaching out should feel simple, professional, and worthwhile.

## Why Canada

### Purpose

Explain regional focus with intention.

### Content role

- Why Canada matters professionally
- Why Andres is aligned with that market
- Signals of seriousness and long-term intent
- Context for employers evaluating international candidates

### Key message

The move toward Canada is strategic, thoughtful, and professionally grounded.

## Future Blog

### Purpose

Build authority and discoverability over time.

### Content role

- Culinary insights
- Professional lessons
- Standards and philosophy
- International career reflections
- Behind-the-work thinking

### Key message

The blog should deepen authority and reveal how Andres thinks, not become a content treadmill.

## Content rules

- Every page must answer a trust question
- Every page must have a clear employer-facing purpose
- Every major block should either humanize, validate, or convert
- Copy should feel premium through clarity, not verbosity
- Content should remain reusable for future multilingual and market-specific adaptations
