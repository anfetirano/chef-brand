# SEO Strategy

## SEO philosophy

SEO should not be treated as traffic chasing. It should be treated as professional discoverability.

The purpose of SEO is to ensure that the right employers, sponsors, collaborators, and recruiters can find trustworthy, relevant information about Andres when they search intentionally or explore related topics.

## Core SEO principles

- Relevance before volume
- Authority before tricks
- Performance before complexity
- Structured clarity before keyword stuffing
- Long-term discoverability before short-term gains

## Future keyword direction

Keyword strategy should combine identity, role, geography, and authority signals. Future exploration may include terms related to:

- Chef Andres and brand-specific search
- International chef opportunities
- Chef in Canada
- Professional chef portfolio
- Executive chef profile
- Culinary experience and specialization
- Hospitality leadership and kitchen standards

Keyword targeting should remain natural and content-led.

## Structured metadata

The platform should eventually support a robust metadata system for:

- Titles
- Descriptions
- Open Graph data
- Canonical URLs
- Page-level relevance
- Asset metadata

Structured metadata should reinforce the site's professional clarity across search engines, social previews, and AI systems.

## International SEO

Because the product is Canada-first but globally extensible, future SEO should support:

- Regional messaging
- Locale-aware structure
- International audience targeting
- Potential multilingual indexing
- Market-specific content without duplication

International SEO should expand deliberately, preserving quality and brand consistency.

## Performance

Performance is part of SEO and part of trust.

Fast loading matters because:

- Search engines reward usable experiences
- Employers judge credibility quickly
- Mobile discovery depends on responsiveness
- A premium brand should never feel slow or unstable

## AI discoverability

The platform should be understandable not only to search engines, but also to AI systems that summarize and recommend content.

This means future content should be:

- Well structured
- Explicit in meaning
- Rich in entity context
- Authentically authored
- Supported by clear metadata

AI discoverability should increase the chance that Andres is surfaced accurately in future search and assistant experiences.

## Content authority

Authority should be built through useful, credible, and consistent content.

Future authority signals may include:

- Strong personal narrative
- Documented experience
- Thoughtful editorial content
- Curated visual proof
- Consistent identity across pages

SEO should reflect real authority, not imitate it.

## SEO guardrails

- No shallow pages created only for ranking
- No manipulative keyword patterns
- No content that weakens brand quality for traffic
- No expansion into topics unrelated to professional opportunity

The SEO strategy should always protect the brand while improving discoverability.
