# Product Roadmap

## Phase 1: Foundation

Define the strategic base of the product:

- Vision
- Principles
- Architecture
- Brand direction
- Content strategy
- SEO strategy
- Analytics strategy
- Delivery philosophy

The output of this phase is alignment, not software.

## Phase 2: Landing

Build the first public trust layer:

- Clear positioning
- Strong first impression
- Core narrative
- Initial employer conversion paths
- Foundational metadata and performance standards

The landing experience should introduce Andres with confidence and purpose.

## Phase 3: Professional Story

Expand beyond a first impression into a deeper narrative:

- Personal story
- Professional philosophy
- Leadership style
- Standards and work ethic
- International ambition

This phase should answer why Andres matters, not only what he has done.

## Phase 4: Gallery

Introduce curated visual proof:

- Food presentation
- Kitchen context
- Professional environment
- Process signals
- Quality and consistency

The gallery should reinforce standards, not function as decoration.

## Phase 5: Resume

Add structured professional proof:

- Experience timeline
- Roles
- Skills
- Certifications
- Downloadable formats

The resume should support trust, not replace the narrative.

## Phase 6: Analytics

Implement measurement capabilities:

- Visitor tracking
- Geographic insights
- Traffic sources
- Conversion signals
- High-interest content analysis

This phase turns the site into a learning system.

## Phase 7: Employer Dashboard

Create a deeper trust environment for qualified viewers:

- Extended professional materials
- Controlled access information
- Richer employer context
- Stronger conversion support

This phase moves from public brand to selective employer enablement.

## Phase 8: CRM

Add opportunity management capabilities:

- Lead organization
- Contact workflows
- Opportunity tracking
- Follow-up support
- Source attribution

This phase connects brand visibility with real business process.

## Phase 9: International Expansion

Prepare the platform for broader global positioning:

- Multilingual content
- Regional pages
- Market-specific messaging
- International SEO
- Global opportunity targeting

At this stage, the platform becomes a durable international professional asset.
